# CPD submission cover (draft for assessors)

This folder contains a structured evidence pack to support a submission to the CPD Certification Service.
It does not claim approval, endorsement, or accreditation.

## Platform overview

RansfordsNotes is a browser-first learning platform that combines structured notes with interactive labs.
It is designed for practitioners who learn by testing ideas and reflecting on outcomes.

## Learning philosophy

- Plain language first, then precision.
- Practical work and interpretation, not memorisation.
- Clear boundaries on what each tool does and does not claim.

## Target audience

- Practitioners and early-career professionals in cybersecurity, AI, software architecture, data, and digitalisation.
- Learners who need clear explanations and structured practice artefacts.
- Teams and educators using browser-based labs for guided learning.

## Delivery method

- Self-paced online delivery through structured course pages and interactive labs.
- Most tools run in the browser. Where server APIs exist, they are used for specific operations only.

## Assessment and reflection approach

- Formative quizzes embedded within courses.
- Practice artefacts produced by tools and dashboards.
- Reflection is supported through prompts and the ability to record learning time and completions.

## Update and quality assurance process

- Course content and tool logic are version-controlled.
- Updates are reviewed and checked through automated lint/build validation.
- CPD governance documents define safe wording and change control posture.

# CPD submission cover (draft)

This pack is prepared to support a submission to CPD Certification Service.
It describes the platform, course structure, assessment approach, evidence approach, and maintenance posture.

## Platform overview

RansfordsNotes is a learning platform combining structured written lessons with interactive, browser-based labs and dashboards.
The intent is to support self-directed professional development with practical exercises and clear learning objectives.

## Learning philosophy

- The platform favours short explanations and direct practice.
- Learners are encouraged to make predictions, test them, and reflect on outcomes.
- The focus is practical competence and judgement, not rote memorisation.

## Target audience

- Practitioners and learners who want structured, hands-on learning
- Professionals who need evidence-friendly CPD activities
- Teams who want repeatable exercises and templates for internal learning

## Delivery method

- Self-paced, online learning
- Written lessons with embedded interactive exercises
- Tools run in the browser where possible

## Assessment and reflection approach

- Formative quizzes embedded within levels
- Interactive tools and dashboards produce structured outputs and artefacts
- Reflection is encouraged through prompts and evidence notes
- No proctoring and no claim of certification through exams

## Evidence tracking approach

Evidence points include:
- level time tracking
- section completion indicators
- quiz completion signals
- tool usage signals where applicable

Evidence is designed to be summarised in a structured, auditable format suitable for CPD records.

## Update and quality assurance process

- Content and tooling are version-controlled
- CI enforces quality and safety guardrails (linting, security checks, key policy checks)
- Security and dependency posture is monitored and updated routinely
- Changes that affect learning objectives or time estimates are reviewed and documented
