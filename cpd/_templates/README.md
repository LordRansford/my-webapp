Shared templates for CPD packs. Use these sections:
- cpd-pack.md: purpose, audience, outcomes, syllabus, delivery, assessment, hours evidence, accessibility, updates, provider, completion rules.
- mapping.(json|md): machine-readable and human-readable mapping of sections to outcomes, assessments, hours.
- quality-and-review.md: cadence, corrections, change control, references, regression prevention.
- assessment.md: thresholds, retakes, labs, completion requirements, integrity.
- provider-and-instructor.md: provider, instructor bio (concise), style, contact.
- policies.md: complaints, privacy, refund placeholder, conduct, accessibility, equal opportunities.
- evidence-checklist.md: quick checklist.
