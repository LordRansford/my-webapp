# Course CPD Summary - Data

## Intended audience
- Beginners and non technical professionals learning data fundamentals
- Practitioners changing role toward data focused work
- Teams refreshing their approach to data quality, governance, and analytics

## Learning outcomes
- Understand what data is, how it is represented, and why quality matters
- Apply knowledge through pipelines, governance choices, and analysis exercises
- Recognise risks such as bias, misuse, and poor interoperability
- Make informed decisions about architecture, controls, and strategic data use

## Learning structure
- Foundations
- Intermediate
- Advanced
- Summary and games

## CPD hours
Estimated hours are provided per level and tracked locally via CPDTracker. Learners record time and activity to support CPD evidence.
