# Foundations Assessment

<SectionProgressToggle courseId="software-architecture" levelId="foundations" sectionId="soft-arch-foundations-assessment" />

Congratulations on completing the Foundations level! This assessment will test your understanding of the core concepts you've learned.

<Callout variant="info" type="info">
**Assessment Details:**
- 20 questions covering all foundation topics
- Pass threshold: 80%
- Time limit: 45 minutes
- Multiple choice and scenario-based questions
</Callout>

## Topics Covered

This assessment evaluates your understanding of:

1. **Discovery & Requirements** - User journeys, NFRs, threat modeling
2. **Architecture Design** - Patterns, principles, and design decisions
3. **Implementation** - Code quality, standards, and best practices
4. **Verification & Testing** - Testing strategies and quality assurance
5. **Deployment & CI/CD** - Deployment patterns and continuous delivery
6. **Operations** - Monitoring, maintenance, and operational excellence
7. **OSI & Diagnostics** - Network troubleshooting and diagnostics
8. **-ilities Framework** - Non-functional requirements and quality attributes
